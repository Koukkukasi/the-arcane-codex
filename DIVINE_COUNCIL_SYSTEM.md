# DIVINE COUNCIL SYSTEM
**The 7 Gods Who Judge Your Actions**
**Date:** 2025-10-30

---

## üé≠ CORE CONCEPT

**7 gods form a divine council that debates your actions and votes on rewards/punishments.**

**Key Principles:**
- Gods speak RARELY (5-10 times per 100 turns) - silence makes divine attention meaningful
- When they speak, it's during MAJOR moments (oaths, forbidden acts, legendary achievements)
- They DISAGREE with each other - create dramatic tension through conflicting divine agendas
- Players earn favor/wrath through gameplay choices (tracked -100 to +100 per god)
- Coalition politics: gods form temporary alliances based on the specific issue

**Innovation:** No other game has gods that actively debate and vote on player actions in real-time.

## üëë THE SEVEN GODS

### 1. **VALDRIS** - God of Order and Justice
**Domain:** Law, oaths, contracts, civilization, honor
**Personality:** Stern judge, values consistency over mercy
**Favors:** Keeping promises, upholding law, protecting innocents, formal justice
**Opposes:** Oath-breaking, chaos, vigilante justice, anarchy
**Quote:** *"The law bends for no one. Not even heroes."*

---

### 2. **KAITHA** - Goddess of Chaos and Freedom
**Domain:** Revolution, creativity, wild magic, breaking chains, unpredictability
**Personality:** Mischievous rebel, finds order suffocating
**Favors:** Breaking unjust laws, creative solutions, risk-taking, defying authority
**Opposes:** Blind obedience, tradition, predictability, conformity
**Quote:** *"Burn the rulebook. Write a better story."*

---

### 3. **MORVANE** - God of Survival and Pragmatism
**Domain:** Self-preservation, adaptation, harsh truths, necessary evils
**Personality:** Cold realist, no room for sentiment
**Favors:** Practical solutions, survival instincts, cutting losses, harsh choices
**Opposes:** Self-sacrifice, idealism, mercy that risks survival
**Quote:** *"The dead have no honor. Survive first, philosophize later."*

---

### 4. **SYLARA** - Goddess of Nature and Balance
**Domain:** Wilderness, cycles, natural law, harmony, primal forces
**Personality:** Patient gardener, sees long view
**Favors:** Protecting nature, respecting balance, honoring cycles, sustainable choices
**Opposes:** Environmental destruction, greed, urban sprawl, unnatural magic
**Quote:** *"The forest remembers. And the forest repays."*

---

### 5. **KORVAN** - God of War and Glory
**Domain:** Combat, honor duels, warrior codes, courage, legendary deeds
**Personality:** Fierce competitor, respects strength
**Favors:** Honorable combat, facing danger head-on, personal glory, warrior oaths
**Opposes:** Cowardice, poison/ambush tactics, surrender, running from fights
**Quote:** *"Face me with steel in hand, or face me not at all."*

---

### 6. **ATHENA** - Goddess of Wisdom and Knowledge
**Domain:** Learning, investigation, logic, truth-seeking, strategy
**Personality:** Patient teacher, values understanding over action
**Favors:** Research, asking questions, learning from mistakes, strategic thinking
**Opposes:** Willful ignorance, book burning, hasty decisions, anti-intellectualism
**Quote:** *"Knowledge is the only treasure that multiplies when shared."*

---

### 7. **MERCUS** - God of Commerce and Ambition
**Domain:** Trade, wealth, social climbing, deals, entrepreneurship
**Personality:** Charming negotiator, everything's transactional
**Favors:** Fair trade, wealth creation, honoring contracts, win-win deals
**Opposes:** Theft (bad business), charity (waste), destroying markets
**Quote:** *"Every problem has a price. Let's negotiate."*

```python
def gather_npc_testimonies(player_action, party_npcs, game_state):
    """
    NPCs testify before divine council votes
    """
    testimonies = []

    for npc in party_npcs:
        approval = npc.approval_score

        # Determine if NPC testifies
        if approval >= 60:
            # High approval: DEFEND
            testimony = {
                'npc': npc.name,
                'stance': 'SUPPORT',
                'text': generate_defense_testimony(npc, player_action),
                'influence': calculate_testimony_influence(npc, 'support')
            }
            testimonies.append(testimony)

        elif approval <= 39:
            # Low approval: CONDEMN
            testimony = {
                'npc': npc.name,
                'stance': 'OPPOSE',
                'text': generate_condemnation_testimony(npc, player_action),
                'influence': calculate_testimony_influence(npc, 'oppose')
            }
            testimonies.append(testimony)

        else:
            # Neutral: ABSTAIN or silence
            pass

    return testimonies

def apply_npc_testimony_influence(god, testimonies):
    """
    Apply NPC testimony influence to god's vote probability
    """
    base_support_chance = calculate_base_support(god, player_action)

    for testimony in testimonies:
        if testimony['stance'] == 'SUPPORT':
            # NPC defending increases support chance
            influence = testimony['influence']

            # Double influence if NPC has high favor with THIS god
            if testimony['npc'].divine_favor[god.id] >= 70:
                influence *= 2

            base_support_chance += influence

        elif testimony['stance'] == 'OPPOSE':
            # NPC condemning decreases support chance
            influence = testimony['influence']
            base_support_chance -= influence

    return base_support_chance
```

```python
def calculate_favor_change(action, god, game_state):
    base_favor = get_base_favor(action, god)

    # Diminishing returns
    times_done = game_state.count_similar_actions(action)
    if times_done > 5:
        base_favor = base_favor * (0.5 ** (times_done - 5))

    # Gods value VARIETY
    if game_state.last_10_actions_all_same():
        base_favor = base_favor * 0.1  # Massive penalty for repetition

    return base_favor
```
