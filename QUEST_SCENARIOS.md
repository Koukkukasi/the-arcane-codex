## üìã Overview

**Each scenario designed with CONFLICTING WHISPERS that create moral dilemmas.**

Every quest features:
- **Public scene** - Both players see same setup
- **Asymmetric player whispers** - Each player gets different moral imperative
- **NPC companions** - 1-2 with hidden agendas
- **NPC whispers** - NPCs share some info, hide personal stakes
- **Multiple solutions** - Collaboration unlocks "best" path, solo action = tragedy
- **Real consequences** - No right answer, just different costs

**Duration:** 45-90 minutes per scenario (15-25 turns)

### Hook:
Grimsby (NPC) owes Thieves Guild 500 gold. They've taken his daughter (age 8) as collateral. He has 3 days to pay or she's sold to slavers.

### The Twist:
Duke's medicine shipment (worth 600g) could save her, BUT it's destined for plague victims (200 people will die without it), AND the medicine is poisoned (sabotage).

### NPC COMPANIONS:

**What Grimsby Hides:**

- That he's willing to let 200 people die to save himself AND his daughter

**Betrayal Trigger:**

**2. RENNA (Vengeful Rogue)**
- **Role:** Scouting expert
- **Strengths:** Lockpicking 75, Stealth 70, knows Thieves Guild layout
- **Fatal Flaw:** IMPULSIVE about Guild - acts without consulting
- **Hidden Agenda:** Guild leader (her brother) ordered the poisoning. She wants to kill him.

**Renna's Whisper (She receives):**

**Grimsby wants:** Save daughter + destroy evidence (confession letter)

**Renna wants:** Kill brother (doesn't care about medicine or child)

**Conflicting Goals = Party Chaos**

---

### SOLUTION PATHS:

**PATH 1: STEAL MEDICINE (Player 1's Imperative)**
- Save Grimsby's daughter (immediate)
- BUT: If distributed, poison kills 200 plague victims
- Grimsby approval +20, but 200 deaths on your conscience
- Divine Council: MORVANE approves (family first), VALDRIS condemns (law broken), SYLARA condemns (healing corrupted)

**PATH 2: REFUSE TO STEAL (Player 2's Imperative)**
- Prevent 200 deaths (greater good)
- BUT: Grimsby's daughter sold to slavers
- Grimsby leaves party in despair, may die trying alone
- Divine Council: VALDRIS approves (law upheld), MORVANE condemns (abandoned child)

**PATH 3: STEAL & WARN (Collaboration Required)**
- Player 1 & 2 share whispers, realize both truths
- Steal medicine BUT immediately warn Duke's healers about poison
- Save daughter, prevent 200 deaths, expose Thieves Guild sabotage
- COST: Confession letter exposed, Grimsby arrested (can be negotiated later)
- Divine Council: ATHENA approves (wisdom), VALDRIS approves (justice), MORVANE neutral

**PATH 4: RESCUE CHILD DIRECTLY (Creative Solution)**
- Ignore medicine, rescue daughter from slavers now (Player 1's whisper reveals guard is vulnerable)
- Combat with slaver (15 HP, 10 attack - winnable fight)
- Save child, medicine still poisoned, 200 may die (Duke's problem)
- Grimsby eternally grateful (Approval 100), confession letter safe
- Divine Council: MORVANE approves (pragmatic), KORVAN approves (direct action), VALDRIS condemns (didn't prevent mass murder)

**PATH 5: EXPOSE SABOTAGE TO GUILD (Political Solution)**
- Player 2 shares poison info, party confronts Thieves Guild about sabotage
- Guild leader (Renna's brother) is exposed
- Renna's impulsive betrayal triggers - she attacks brother
- IF party helps Renna: Brother dies, Guild becomes enemy, but respects strength
- IF party stops Renna: Brother lives, negotiates daughter's release for exposing poison plot
- Daughter saved, 200 saved, but Guild civil war starts

---

### TURN-BY-TURN OUTLINE:

**Turn 1:** Grimsby's plea, public scene
**Turn 2:** Asymmetric whispers, party planning
**Turn 3:** Travel to warehouse
**Turn 4:** Reconnaissance (Renna scouts, spots brother)
**Turn 5:** Entry decision (which path?)
**Turns 6-10:** Heist/combat/negotiation (depends on choice)
**Turns 11-15:** Consequence resolution
**Final Turn:** Divine Council judges party's choice

**If Renna kills brother:**
