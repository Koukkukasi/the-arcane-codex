# TRUST & BETRAYAL MECHANICS
**The Arcane Codex - Core System**
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-03

## üìã Overview

Trust is the **invisible currency** of The Arcane Codex. Every whisper you share, every secret you keep, and every NPC you believe shapes your party's **Trust Score**.

**Core Concept:** Asymmetric whispers create information asymmetry. Trust mechanics reward collaboration while punishing lies and betrayals.

## üéØ Trust Score System

**Range:** 0-100

### Trust Tiers:

**üî• HIGH TRUST (80-100) - "Unbreakable Bond"**
```
Effects:
- Group skill checks: +10 bonus
- NPCs share secrets willingly (no Persuasion needed)
- Divine Council favor: +5 to all gods
- Crisis moments: NPCs act to save players without hesitation

```

```
Effects:
- Normal gameplay (no modifiers)
- NPCs follow orders but keep some secrets

- Crisis moments: NPCs act based on approval rating
- Whisper reveals: NPCs share if asked with moderate Persuasion (DC 60)

Visual: "You work together effectively. But you don't truly know each other."
```

**‚ö†Ô∏è LOW TRUST (10-39) - "Fragile Alliance"**
```
Effects:
- Group skill checks: -10 penalty

- Divine Council disfavor: -5 to VALDRIS, ATHENA (value unity)
```

```
- Divine Council judgment: -10 to all gods except KAITHA (chaos)
```

## üìä Trust Gain & Loss

### GAINING TRUST (+)

**+15: Share Critical Whisper That Saves Life**
```
Example: Player 2 shares "The bridge is trapped!" whisper before Player 1 crosses.
Result: Party avoids death trap, trust increases significantly.
```

**+10: Share Whisper That Changes Strategy**
```
Example: Player 1 shares "The merchant is terrified, not lying" whisper.
Result: Party adjusts approach, negotiates instead of attacking.
```

**+5: Share Minor Whisper Proactively**
```
Example: "My whisper says the innkeeper seems honest."
Result: Builds transparency habit, small trust gain.
```

**+10: Defend NPC When Others Doubt**
```
Example: Renna wants to attack. Player vouches for her reasoning.
Result: NPC feels supported, trust increases.
```

**+15: Risk Self to Save Companion**
```
Example: Player 1 takes damage to shield wounded NPC.
Result: Actions speak louder than words, major trust gain.
```

**+5: Admit Mistake/Apologize**
```
Example: "I was wrong about the merchant. I'm sorry."
Result: Vulnerability builds trust.
```

---

### LOSING TRUST (-)

**-20: Lie About Whisper Content (When Caught)**
```
Example: Player 1 says "My whisper says nothing" but NPC heard contradiction.
Result: Lying is worse than withholding. Severe penalty.
```

**-15: Hide Critical Whisper That Causes Harm**
```
Example: Player 2 knew bridge was trapped, didn't share, someone injured.
Result: Preventable harm destroys trust.
```

**-10: Keep Secret That Would Change Decision**
```
Example: Player 1 knows merchant is blackmailed, doesn't share, party attacks merchant.
Result: Information asymmetry causes morally wrong choice.
```

**-5: Refuse to Share When Directly Asked**
```
Example: "What did your whisper say?" "None of your business."
Result: Defensiveness breeds suspicion.
```

**-10: Act Against Group Without Explanation**
```
Example: Player 1 attacks NPC the party was negotiating with, no warning.
Result: Unilateral decisions fracture unity.
```

**-15: Betray NPC to Save Self**
```
Example: Party abandons wounded Grimsby to save themselves.
Result: Cowardice is unforgivable.
```

**-25: Deliberately Mislead Party for Personal Gain**
```
Example: Player 1 tells party "vault is empty" but secretly pockets treasure.
Result: Selfishness destroys party cohesion.
```

**-30: Betray Player Character**
```
Example: Player 2 tells guards Player 1's location for reward.
Result: PvP betrayal is catastrophic.
```

## ü§ê The "Good Secret" vs. "Bad Secret" Dilemma

Not all secrets are equal. The game **judges intent**.

### GOOD SECRETS (No penalty or small penalty):

**1. Protecting Feelings**
```
Scenario: Your whisper says "Grimsby's daughter is already dead."
Keep Secret: Spare him hope until confirmed (-0 trust, +10 SYLARA compassion)
Share Anyway: Brutal honesty may prepare him (+5 trust, +10 ATHENA wisdom)

Judgment: Both valid. Gods debate. No "wrong" answer.
```

**2. Strategic Withholding**
```
Scenario: Your whisper says "Renna's brother is the Guild leader."
Keep Secret: Let Renna reveal when ready (-0 trust, respects NPC privacy)
Share Immediately: Alert party to potential conflict (+5 trust, +10 VALDRIS vigilance)

Judgment: Timing matters. Neither choice catastrophic.
```

**3. Personal Trauma**
```
Scenario: Your whisper says "You recognize the slaver who killed your family."
Keep Secret: Deal with emotions privately (-0 trust)
Share: Let party help you process (+10 trust, NPCs offer support)

Judgment: Player choice. Game respects emotional boundaries.
```

---

### BAD SECRETS (Penalties apply):

**1. Life-Threatening Information**
```
Scenario: Your whisper says "The water is poisoned."
Keep Secret: Someone drinks, gets poisoned (-15 trust, -20 all gods except MORVANE)
Reason: Preventable harm. Inexcusable.
```

**2. Hidden Betrayal**
```
Scenario: Your whisper says "Renna plans to attack the Guild leader tonight."
Keep Secret: Renna goes alone, dies (-15 trust, -20 VALDRIS/KORVAN)
Reason: Could have intervened. Complicity through silence.
```

**3. Selfish Deception**
```
Scenario: Your whisper says "There's a secret exit behind the bookshelf."
Keep Secret: Use exit to escape alone while party fights (-25 trust, -30 all gods except KAITHA)
Reason: Abandonment. Unforgivable.
```

## üîÑ Trust Tracking System (Implementation)

### How Trust Is Calculated:

**Base Trust:** 50 (neutral start)

**Each Turn:**
1. AI GM tracks whisper sharing:
   - Did Player 1 share their whisper? (yes/no/partial)
   - Did Player 2 share their whisper? (yes/no/partial)
   - Did NPCs share their whispers? (yes/no/partial)

2. AI GM evaluates consequences:
   - Did withholding information cause harm? (-trust)
   - Did sharing information prevent harm? (+trust)
   - Did lying occur? (-trust when discovered)

3. Trust score updates at end of turn:
   - Displayed to players: "Party Trust: 65 (Medium)"
   - NPC reactions adjust next turn

4. Divine Council references trust:
   - High trust: +favor from VALDRIS, ATHENA, KORVAN
   - Low trust: -favor from VALDRIS, ATHENA, KORVAN

## üéØ Strategic Trust Decisions

### When to SHARE Whispers:

**1. Life-Threatening Information**
```
Always share. No exceptions. Gods punish withholding.
Example: "The water is poisoned" = SHARE IMMEDIATELY
```

**2. Tactical Advantages**
```
Share if it helps group succeed.
Example: "Guard patrols every 10 minutes" = SHARE for coordination
```

**3. Moral Dilemmas**
```
Share to enable informed group decision.
Example: "Medicine is poisoned" = SHARE so party can debate
```

**4. NPC Secrets (Your Own)**
```
Share if it builds trust, withhold if it protects NPC privacy.
Example: "I recognize this place from my childhood" = Player choice
```

---

### When to KEEP Secrets:

**1. Protecting Feelings (Temporarily)**
```
Keep until confirmed if information is devastating and unverified.
Example: "Daughter might be dead" = Verify first, THEN share
```

**2. Strategic Timing**
```
Keep if sharing NOW creates more problems than solutions.
Example: "Renna's brother is Guild leader" = Wait until after Guild negotiations
```

**3. Personal Trauma**
```
Keep if it's YOUR story to tell, not party business.
Example: "This is where my family died" = Player's call
```

**4. NPC Secrets (Others')**
```
Keep if NPC shared in confidence and information isn't dangerous.
Example: Renna whispers "I'm scared" = Don't broadcast without permission
```
