================================================================================

All transitions use: ease timing function

  General UI: 0.3s ease
    - Button hover

    - Filter changes

    - Close button color

    - Close button: 90-degree rotation

  Equipment Slot: scale(1.05)
  Inventory Slot: scale(1.08)
  Button: Color & glow change
  Close Button: 90-degree rotate
