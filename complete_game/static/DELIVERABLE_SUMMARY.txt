================================================================================

  Change to: <div class="character-name">YOUR_NAME</div>

Update Stat Value:
  Find: <div class="stat-value">18</div>
  Change to: <div class="stat-value">NEW_VALUE</div>

Modify XP Progress:
  Find: <div class="xp-fill" style="width: 65%;"></div>
  Change to: <div class="xp-fill" style="width: XX%;"></div>
  Also update: <div class="xp-text">CURRENT / MAXIMUM (XX%)</div>

Update Divine Favor:
  Find: <div class="divine-fill valdris-bar" style="width: 60%;"></div>
  Change to: style="width: XX%;" (for percentage)
  And update: <div class="divine-value">+15</div> (the number)
