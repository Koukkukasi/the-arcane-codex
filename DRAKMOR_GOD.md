# DRAKMOR - The Dragon-God
**The Arcane Codex - 8th Divine Entity**
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-01

## üìã Overview

**DRAKMOR** (DRAK-mor) - Ancient Dragon-God of **Freedom, Primal Fury, and Transformation**

**Domain:** Independence, raw power, territorial instinct, shapeshifting, draconic magic, wolves as pack mentality, transformation through conflict

**Symbol:** Dragon and Wolf intertwined in eternal spiral

**Followers:** Dragon riders, wolf shamans, shapeshifters, berserkers, those who reject civilization's chains, wilderness guardians

---

## üé≠ Personality & Philosophy

### Core Beliefs:

**"Civilization is a cage. Power is truth. Transform or perish."**

DRAKMOR represents the **primal self** - the part of mortals that rejects rules, hierarchies, and social constraints. Neither wholly good nor evil, DRAKMOR embodies **raw authenticity** and the freedom to be what you truly are.

**Key Principles:**
1. **Independence over obedience** - No master, no chains
2. **Strength through conflict** - You become strong by fighting, not by peace
3. **Territory is sacred** - Protect what's yours with fury
4. **Pack loyalty** - Few bonds, but unbreakable
5. **Transformation is natural** - Change form, change self, evolve

**Opposes:**
- VALDRIS (Order constrains freedom)
- MERCUS (Commerce enslaves through debt)
- Social hierarchies, laws, taxation, "civilization"

**Respects:**
- KAITHA (Fellow chaos god, but DRAKMOR is purposeful chaos)
- MORVANE (Survival instinct)
- SYLARA (Nature's freedom)

## ‚öñÔ∏è Divine Favor System

**Range:** -100 (Enslaver) to +100 (Wild Soul)

### Gaining Favor (+):
- **+30:** Reject authority to follow own path
- **+25:** Defend territory/loved ones with primal fury
- **+20:** Transform self (physical/emotional breakthrough)
- **+15:** Choose freedom over safety/wealth
- **+10:** Show loyalty to small pack over large society
- **+10:** Embrace beast/dragon nature without shame
- **+5:** Refuse to be tamed or controlled

### Losing Favor (-):
- **-30:** Enslave others or strip their freedom
- **-25:** Submit to unjust authority out of cowardice
- **-20:** Betray pack/family for personal gain
- **-15:** Cage/chain wild creatures for profit
- **-10:** Choose civilization's comfort over authentic self
- **-10:** Mock those who embrace primal nature
- **-5:** Follow rules without questioning why

## üéÅ Divine Blessings (At +70 Favor)

### 1. **DRAGON'S BLOOD** (Physical Transformation)
```
Your veins burn with draconic essence.

Effects:
- Immune to fire damage
- Unarmed attacks deal +10 damage (claws manifest)
- Intimidation +20 (draconic presence)
- Social penalty in civilized areas (people fear you)

Duration: Until favor drops below +50

Roleplay: Your eyes glow amber. Scales shimmer on your skin. Voice deepens to guttural growl.
```

### 2. **WOLF'S PACK** (Companion Manifestation)
```
A dire wolf companion appears, bound to your soul.

Effects:
- Dire wolf fights with you (30 HP, 20 attack)
- Wolf can scout ahead (Perception +30)
- Wolf senses danger (warns of ambushes)
- If wolf dies, respawns after 24 hours (real time)

Duration: Until favor drops below +50

Roleplay: Massive gray wolf, scarred and fierce. Loyal only to you. Growls at strangers.
```

### 3. **SHAPESHIFTER'S GIFT** (Ultimate Transformation)
```
Once per session, transform into dragon or wolf form.

Dragon Form (10 minutes):
- Fly, breathe fire (30 damage cone)
- HP +50, all damage +20
- Cannot use items/weapons
- Terrifying to mortals

Wolf Form (1 hour):
- Speed x3, stealth +40
- Track by scent (Survival +30)
- Pack tactics (if with allies)
- Bite attack (15 damage)

Cooldown: One transformation per session

Roleplay: Painful, ecstatic transformation. Bones crack, skin ripples. You BECOME the beast.
```

## üíÄ Divine Curses (At -50 Favor)

### 1. **THE CAGED FURY** (Impotent Rage)
```
Your inner beast is caged. You feel its fury but cannot unleash it.

Effects:
- All attack damage -30%
- Cannot enter rage/berserk states
- Constant frustration (Wisdom checks -10)
- Animals flee from you (unnatural presence)

Duration: Until favor rises above -30

Roleplay: You want to rage but feel hollow. The fire inside has turned to cold ash.
```

### 2. **THE BROKEN PACK** (Isolation)
```
Those you love instinctively distance themselves from you.

Effects:
- All companion approval -20
- Pack animals (wolves, dogs) attack you on sight
- Allies hesitate to help (must roll Persuasion to request aid)
- Loneliness gnaws at you (morale penalties)

Duration: Until favor rises above -30

Roleplay: Your presence feels wrong. Friends avoid eye contact. Even your shadow seems to shrink away.
```

### 3. **THE TAMED SLAVE** (Submission Compulsion)
```
DRAKMOR has marked you as broken. Authorities sense your submission.

Effects:
- Cannot disobey direct orders from authority figures
- Guards/nobles automatically intimidate you (no roll)
- Lost 50% gold to "taxes" when entering cities
- Other gods view you with contempt

Duration: Until favor rises above -30

Roleplay: You flinch at raised voices. Your spine bends instinctively. The wild soul inside is dying.
```

   - DRAKMOR: -10 (compromises with oppressor)

   - DRAKMOR: +25 (respects trial by strength)
   - KORVAN: +25 (honor)

## üêâ Dragons & Wolves Connection

**Why Dragons AND Wolves?**

**Dragons represent:**
- **SOLITARY freedom** - The lone apex predator who needs no one
- **Territorial fury** - "This mountain is MINE"
- **Transformation** - Ancient, powerful, evolving

**Wolves represent:**
- **PACK freedom** - Free together, strong together
- **Loyal fury** - "Touch my pack and DIE"
- **Hunting instinct** - Survival through cooperation

**DRAKMOR embodies BOTH:**
- The **dragon** who needs no master
- The **wolf** who protects its pack unto death

**Players favored by DRAKMOR can become:**
- Dragon Riders (solitary power)
- Wolf Shamans (pack leaders)
- Shapeshifters (embrace both natures)
